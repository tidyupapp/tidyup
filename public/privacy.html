Privacy Policy

Last updated:

⚠️ This is a prototype. This policy is a placeholder. Before any wide launch, replace this with a real document reviewed by a lawyer.

What we collect

  • From Google sign-in: your name, email, and profile picture URL.
  • From you: photos you upload, listing titles, descriptions, prices, categories. Stored on your device and on our servers tied to your account.
  • From marketplace connections (eBay, etc.): OAuth tokens for posting on your behalf, plus listing status returned by their APIs.

What we DON'T collect

  • Your password — sign-in is via Google.
  • Other users' marketplace data — we never scrape platforms.
  • Your location (unless you opt in for local-marketplace targeting).
  • Contacts, calendar, or other phone data.

How we use it

  • To send your photo to Google's Gemini AI (or Anthropic's Claude) so it can draft a resale listing. The AI provider sees the photo but does not retain it for training.
  • To save your listings so they appear in your dashboard.
  • To post listings to connected marketplaces (eBay, etc.) when you tap Post.

Who we share with

Only the third parties strictly necessary to provide the service:

  • Google (Firebase Auth): handles your sign-in.
  • Google (Gemini API) or Anthropic (Claude API): AI for listing generation.
  • Cloudflare: hosts our app and database.
  • Connected marketplaces: when you publish a listing to them.

We do not sell your data. We do not share with advertisers. We do not run third-party tracking scripts.

Your data, your control

  • You can disconnect any marketplace any time from the Connections page.
  • You can sign out at any time.
  • You can request account deletion via the in-app feedback option (coming soon). We delete within 30 days.

Cookies

We use cookies and localStorage only to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. No analytics, no ad cookies.

Contact

Use the in-app feedback option (coming soon). We deliberately do not publish personal contact information — feedback comes through the app so we can route it properly.
