What Tidyup does

Tidyup helps you sell items from around your home by drafting resale listings from a photo using AI, and either posting them automatically (for platforms with developer APIs like eBay) or preparing the content for you to paste manually on platforms without developer APIs (Facebook Marketplace, OfferUp, Nextdoor, Craigslist).

What Tidyup is NOT

  • We are not affiliated with eBay, Facebook, Meta, OfferUp, Nextdoor, Craigslist, Etsy, Mercari, Poshmark, Depop, Vinted, or any other marketplace.
  • Logos and platform names are trademarks of their respective owners.
  • We do not post on your behalf for platforms where we do not have an official API connection. You always perform the final action.

Your account

You sign in with Google. We do not store your password. You may sign out or delete your account at any time. Deleting your account removes all stored data within 30 days.

Marketplace connections

When you connect a marketplace (e.g. eBay) via OAuth, we store the access tokens that platform issues us so we can post on your behalf. You can disconnect any time, which revokes our access and deletes the tokens from our database.

Data

We never sell your data. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Limitations

Tidyup is provided as-is. We make no guarantees that any listing will sell, that AI-generated descriptions will be accurate, or that marketplace integrations will remain available (platforms change their APIs at their discretion).
